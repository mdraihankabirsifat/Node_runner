CORE RULES

How to survive

01
Claim a free node

A new node instantly restores your heart. One player fits in each node.

02
Never camp

Your heart drains quickly while occupying a node, so rotate before it reaches zero.

03
Manage exposure

Your exposure timer rises outside nodes and pauses inside. Higher exposure makes the arena more dangerous.

04
Use the center

The center heals slowly, but exposure keeps increasing. It is a recovery stop—not permanent safety.

05
Be the last alive

After each elimination the arena shrinks and one node disappears. The final geometry stays at least triangular.
