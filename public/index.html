CORE RULES

How to survive

1
Race for a node

There is always one fewer active node than alive runners.

2
Keep rotating

A different node restores health to 100. Staying in a node drains health quickly.

3
Manage two stats

Health reaches zero or the exposure timer reaches its limit: you are eliminated.

4
Use the center carefully

The center restores health slowly, but your timer continues increasing.

5
Last runner wins

After every elimination, the arena shrinks and one node disappears.
